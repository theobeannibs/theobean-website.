A Chain of Trust, Not a Supply Chain

Born from a family story of injustice, Theobean was created to build a direct bridge from the grove to the global market. We use technology to ensure value stays where it belongs: with the source. This is more than fair trade; it's economic sovereignty.

The Founder's Ledger

Our first supporters are the bedrock of this mission. The Founder's Ledger is a permanent, digital monument to the patrons and allies who helped bring Theobean to life. Their names are inscribed as a testament to their belief in a more equitable future.
